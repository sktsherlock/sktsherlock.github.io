    My name is Hao Yan. Currently, I am a Phd Student in the Department of Computer Science at Central South University under the supervision of Professor Senzhang Wang. I received my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Hefei University of Technology in 2021. From 2021 to present, I study in BigData 4 Urban Intelligence Lab lead by Professor Senzhang Wang. From August 2022 to January 2023, I interned in the Social Computing Group at Microsoft Research Asia, under the supervision of Senior Researcher Chaozhuo Li and focused on text-attributed graphs domain. From February 2023 to January 2024, I served as a remote research intern at Microsoft STCA, tracking research related to representation learning methods on text-attributed graphs.

    My main research interests lie in

    • Graph Neural Networks, Graph Contrastive Learning, Self-supervised Learning on Graphs
    • Text-attributed Graphs, Multimodal attributed Graphs
    • Large Language Model, LLMs for Graph
